Da Nang

The city has changed rapidly with a terrifying development speed, which is the comment of most people when they return to this beach city. Da Nang is located in the Central Part of Vietnam, so it is very convenient for people from the North or the South to move to. With a great location and a combination of sightseeing with Hoi An and Hue, Da Nang attracts millions of domestic and international tourists every year.
